About Us

Why Circlemesh?

As regular attendees at events, we noticed that despite being surrounded by amazing individuals it is really hard to identify those that match what we're looking for at that event. 

For example, most of the times we go to an event thinking we might meet with someone who works at Company X or has Profile Y. Even if there are individuals that match that exact description, there is no easy way to identify them in the room (other than talking to everyone, which is not humanly possible for most events). Based on our research, most attendees face this issue. That's why we decided to build CircleMesh and solve this problem.
